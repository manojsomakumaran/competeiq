# AI-Driven Multi-Agent E-Commerce Competitive Intelligence System

## Introduction & Motivation

The integration of Generative AI (GenAI) and Large Language Models (LLMs) has significantly transformed e-commerce operations by enabling:

- Advanced competitive analysis  
- Automated pricing intelligence  
- Dynamic marketing content generation  

AI-powered solutions help:

- Monitor competitor strategies  
- Identify market opportunities  
- Optimize positioning  

However, extracting actionable intelligence from competitor catalogs and pricing remains a challenge.

**CompeteIQ** addresses this by leveraging LLMs and multi-agent architectures.
